Greener choices...

As the use of adaptogens (non-toxic plants) and the legal use of marijuana and other cannabis-derived products grows, consumers are becoming more curious about their options. CBD is found primarily in extractions from the hemp plant. It’s sold in gels, gummies, oils, supplements, extracts, and more. THC is the main psychoactive compound in marijuana that gives the high sensation. It can be consumed by smoking marijuana. It’s also available in oils, edibles, tinctures, capsules, and more. Both compounds interact with your body’s endocannabinoid system, but they have very different effects.
